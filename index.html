The problem

Analytics work gets messy when nobody owns the middle.

  • Business teams need answers, but dashboards depend on tracking, mappings, data exports, access, platform quirks and vendor timelines.
  • Technical teams need clear requirements, but requests often arrive as screenshots, vague metric definitions or urgent discrepancy questions.
  • Leadership loses confidence when numbers change without explanation, QA evidence or documented ownership.
  • I sit in that middle and turn the chaos into clear work, visible decisions, QA checks and usable reporting.
